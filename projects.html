Project Title Duration Description
An Intelligent Approach to the State of Charge (SoC) estimation January 2022 to May 2022

This project explores the various existing methodologies to state of charge estimation in Li-ion
batteries and weighs out the pros and cons of the same. Finally, the project proposes a novel
Deep Feedforward Neural Network (DFNN) approach and the comparative analysis is carried out.

Optimization Of MPPT Curve Considering Nonlinearities
Using Evolutionary Algorithms 		August 2021 to December 2021

The performance of various optimization algorithms was tested by tracking of maximum power point (MPP)
via the means of custom testbench functions and MATLAB Global Optimization Toolbox.

Detection Of Covid-19 Using Lung X-Ray January 2021 to May 2021

CNN was deployed using Keras framework with the Tensorflow backend. The images were pre-processed and
basic exploratory data analysis (EDA) was carried out.
